Buttons

Use these Tailwind CSS button components in your Angular application to surface the different actions someone can perform in your interface.

Primary buttons

Primary buttons have the most visual impact after the FAB, and should be used for important, final actions that complete a flow, like Save, Join now, or Confirm.

Secondary buttons

Outlined buttons are medium-emphasis buttons. They contain actions that are important, but aren’t the primary action in an app.

Tonal buttons

A tonal button is an alternative middle ground between primary and secondary buttons. They’re useful in contexts where a lower-priority button requires slightly more emphasis than a secondary would give, such as "Next" in an onboarding flow.
